# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Hiring managers, senior engineers, and ML reviewers who need to evaluate whether
GAN Image Studio is a credible portfolio project rather than a toy demo. They
arrive with limited time, scan for evidence of engineering judgment, and need a
clear path from the visual result to the code, tests, and reproducibility story.

## Product Purpose

GAN Image Studio demonstrates a functional DCGAN training pipeline with dataset
inspection, checkpointing, inference, evaluation, and an exploration interface.
The portfolio surface should make the project easy to judge quickly: what it
does, how it is structured, what has been verified, and what quality claims are
intentionally not being made yet.

## Brand Personality

Precise, experimental, and accountable. The voice should feel like a lab bench
kept clean enough for another engineer to reproduce the work.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI startup landing pages, fake model-quality claims, decorative
neon dashboards, glassy SaaS cards, and portfolio pages that hide engineering
substance behind vague slogans.

## Design Principles

- Show artifacts before adjectives.
- Make verification visible without turning the page into documentation.
- Treat uncertainty as credibility: label smoke-test outputs and limitations.
- Keep interaction focused on inspection, not spectacle.
- Let the codebase's separations of concern shape the page narrative.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-operable controls, descriptive alt text for
all meaningful generated-image artifacts, and reduced-motion alternatives for
any transform-based animation.
